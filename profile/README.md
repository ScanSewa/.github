## 🚀 What we build

**ScanSewa** is an all-in-one platform that helps food & hospitality businesses run their **entire operation** from a single, cloud-connected system — billing, orders, kitchen, menu, inventory, staff, customers, and hotel rooms — with real-time sync across every device.

From a busy walk-in café to a full-service hotel, ScanSewa replaces the tangle of separate tools with **one login, one dashboard, everything in sync**.

<div align="center">

`Take orders` → `Fire to kitchen` → `Bill & pay` → `Auto-deplete stock` → `See profit` — in real time.

</div>

## 🧩 The platform

| Product | For whom | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 🧾 **Vendor ERP & POS** | Restaurant owners | Billing, orders, tables, menu, KOT/KDS, inventory, recipes, purchases, finance & sales insights |
| 📱 **Customer App** | Diners & guests | QR-code menu, self-ordering, and live order tracking |
| 🧑‍🍳 **Captain App** | Waiters & riders | Take table orders and manage deliveries on the go |
| 🛎️ **Hotel PMS** | Hotels & stays | Rooms, front-desk, housekeeping, and guest bookings |
| 🛠️ **Master Panel** | ScanSewa admins | Vendor onboarding, plans, add-ons & platform control |
| 🌐 **Website** | Everyone | Discover ScanSewa and get started |
